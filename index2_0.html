ABOUT

We are Greenland Holiday Inn is a serviced villa providing accommodation to the travellers around whole Wayanad. Situated in Puzhamudi, a small village just 3 km from Kalpetta. We have 8 rooms, out of which 4 superior rooms and 4 economy rooms. We are providing kitchen facilities, dining and campfire at affordable rates. The property is sorrounded by plantations and the scenery from here is amazing. We are trying to bringing back your nostalgic moments and this is a perfect place where your destination meets. Our staff is always dedicated to serve you better. Special rates are available for travel agents, company staff etc..
